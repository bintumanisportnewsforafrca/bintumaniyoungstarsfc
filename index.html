Our Story
Bintumani Young Stars Football Club began its journey in 2017 under the leadership of Coach Kanayo (Sadiku Sallieu Fofanah). The club‚Äôs name evolved from Thankorosidia F.C., to Madina F.C., and then Mantenkelly F.C. Scout and develop young football talent
