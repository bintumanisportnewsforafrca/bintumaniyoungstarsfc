Our Story
Bintumani Young Stars Football Club began its journey in 2017 under the leadership of Coach Kanayo. The name evolved from Thankorosidia F.C., to Madina F.C., and then Mantenkelly F.C. It was later changed to Bintumani Young Stars due to legal requirements on community club names.

Our Mission

  • Scout and develop young football talent
  • Create opportunities on national and international stages
  • Uplift young players from street life through sport
  • Build discipline, confidence, and career pathways
